Grain size analysis

Surface and subsurface sedimentological data stemming from grain size analysis of sieved fractions are inputs for the River Analyst database. Thus , River Analyst features a web app for grain size analysis, Sediment Analyst, to produce quick, interactive, and visual sedimentological analyses, and to output summary statistics in form of CSV tables, which can be used to populate the River Analyst database.
